About Project


Walletance is a finance mobile app that helps you to get more control on your money. It is designed to allow you to plan your monthly budget and makes tracking of your spendings more easier and clearer. Thanks to the application, you can check very accurate parameters of your budget, make transfers, connect any number of bank accounts and cards.
